Unqualified

Approved

Other requirements to work as an unqualified member of staff

No qualifications are required to work as an unqualified member of staff in an early years setting. However, as set out in the early years foundation stage (EYFS) statutory framework, providers must ensure that staffing arrangements meet the needs of all children and ensure their safety.

Providers must make sure that their members of staff meet all other requirements as set out in the EYFS and the early years qualification requirements and standards document.

Level 2

Approved

Other requirements to count in the level 2 staff to child ratios

As this qualification was achieved after 30 June 2016, the member of staff must obtain a paediatric first aid qualification which meets the criteria in the early years foundation stage (EYFS) statutory framework within 3 months of starting work. To continue to be included in the ratio requirement the certificate must be renewed every 3 years.

Providers must make sure that their members of staff meet all other requirements as set out in the EYFS and the early years qualification requirements and standards document.

Level 3

Approved

Other requirements to count in the level 3 staff to child ratios
For someone who holds this qualification to be included in the staff to child ratios at level 3, they must:

  • hold a suitable level 2 English qualification, as specified in the early years qualification requirements and standards document
  • obtain a paediatric first aid qualification which meets the criteria in the early years foundation stage (EYFS) statutory framework
    • within 3 months of starting work. To continue to be included in the ratio requirement the certificate must be renewed every 3 years.
  • meet all other requirements as set out in the EYFS and the early years qualification requirements and standards document

A person who holds a full and relevant level 3 qualification started after September 2014, but who does not hold a suitable level 2 qualification in English can be counted in the staff to child ratios at level 2.

Providers must make sure that their members of staff meet all other requirements as set out in the EYFS and the early years qualification requirements and standards document.

Level 6

Not approved

Requirements to work at level 6
To be included in the staff to child ratios at level 6, staff must hold one of the following:

  • Qualified Teacher Status
  • Early Years Teacher Status
  • Early Years Professional Status (EYPS)

Staff who hold one of these qualifications may be counted in the staff to child ratios at either level 3 or level 6, but not both.

Providers must make sure that their members of staff meet all other requirements as set out in the EYFS and the early years qualification requirements and standards document.
