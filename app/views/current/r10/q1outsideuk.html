Qualifications achieved outside the United Kingdom

If you did not get your qualification in the United Kingdom you will have to make an application for recognition to work in early years education in England.


The early years qualifications achieved outside the United Kingdom guidance covers the information you will need to apply for recognition of overseas qualifications to be counted in the EYFS staff to child ratios in England.


This applies to those in:

  • Europe
  • Rest of the world
