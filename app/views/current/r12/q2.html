Why do we ask for the date the qualification was started?

DfE sets the criteria for qualifications to be approved as 'full and relevant'. These criteria occasionally change. We use the date the qualification was started, rather than the completion date, to establish which criteria the qualification was approved against.

You can read more about the criteria in the early years qualification requirements and standards statutory guidance (opens in new tab).
