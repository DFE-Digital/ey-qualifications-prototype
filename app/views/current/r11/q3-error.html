How do I find out what level it is?

Most qualifications have a difficulty level. The higher the level, the more difficult the qualification is.

To find the level of a qualification, you can:

  • check the qualification certificate
  • ask the person who holds the qualification
  • see examples of qualification levels in England, Wales and Northern Ireland
