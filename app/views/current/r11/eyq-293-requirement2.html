What is Quality Assurance Agency for Higher Education (QAA)?
The Quality Assurance Agency for Higher Education (QAA) Subject Benchmark Statement for Early Childhood Studies describes what graduates might reasonably be expected to know at the end of their studies.
The university or training provider should be able to confirm if the course was consistent with the Statement.
