I cannot find the qualification on the list

If you cannot find the qualification on the list, it may still be considered full and relevant if it is included in a group of qualifications.

Grouped qualifications for level 6

The list of level 6 qualifications on the EYQL includes the following grouped qualifications:

BA (Hons) Early Childhood Studies

If the qualification is a BA (Hons) or bachelor of arts, and the title includes Early Childhood Studies, please choose this qualification.

Check a BA (Hons) Early Childhood Studies qualification

Degrees and honours degrees at Level 6

Many universities offer degrees in early years subjects. You can choose this option if:

  • the qualification was awarded by a university or higher education institution in England
  • the qualification is related to early years
  • the qualification name includes one of the following:
    • BA, BA (Hons) or Bachelor of Arts
    • BEd, BEd (Hons) or Bachelor of Education
    • BSc, BSc (Hons) or Bachelor of Science
