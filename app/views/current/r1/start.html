{{ serviceName }}

Use this service to:

  • check if an early years qualification is approved by the Department for Education (DfE) as full and relevant
  • confirm if someone who holds this qualification can count in staff to child ratios, as set out in the early years foundation stage (EYFS)

To be approved as full and relevant, the qualification name and awarding body must exactly match the entry on the early years qualifications list (EYQL), including any wording in brackets.

A qualification may have further conditions which have to be met before it can be considered full and relevant. This service will show you if any conditions apply to the qualification you check.

Before you start

This service will ask you about:

  • the qualification name
  • the awarding organisation for the qualification
  • the date that the qualification was started

You may find it helpful to have this information to hand. The qualification name and awarding organisation can usually be found on the qualification certificate.

Get more help and advice

If you need more help, contact Ecctis. Ecctis provides qualification checking guidance on behalf of DfE. Information on the guidance Ecctis is able to provide can be found on its website.
