This qualification is approved by the Department for Education (DfE) as full and relevant, subject to the following notes:

  • The qualification must be fully consistent with the QAA subject benchmark statement for Early Childhood Studies
  • The qualification must include an element of assessed practice in an early years setting.
  • Even though this is a level 6 qualification, someone who holds this qualification can only be included in the staff:child ratios at level 3.

You must make sure that the qualification name exactly matches the qualification on the certificate, including any wording in brackets.

This is a level 6 qualification which was started after 1 September 2014. Someone who holds this qualification can be included in staff:child ratios at level 3.

To be included in the staff:child ratios at level 6, staff must hold one of the following or another approved level 6 qualification:

  • Qualified Teacher Status (QTS)
  • Early Years Teacher Status (EYTS)
  • Early Years Professional Status (EYPS)
